Born and raised in the Grosse Pointe and Detroit areas, Elizabeth Lynn Marck has always shown an interest in books and literature, and eventually went to gradute from her high school, Grosse Pointe North, as valdevictorian. Her achievements in her high school career carried her to the prestigous University of Michigan, where she would continue to make a name for herself: she graduated in December 2016 with a major in English and a minor in history after spending only two and a half years at the university.



Elizabeth currently works under the direction of David Aretha as a freelance editor.
